How to Vote

Online Voting

1. Check Your Email

If you registered by January 31st, you should have received your ballot information by email. Check your spam folder if you don't see it. Alternatively, you should have received a physical letter (delivered by USPS) from CADEM with your ballot information.

2. Vote for Our Slate

Vote for all 14 candidates on the Democrats for Progress and Justice Slate.

3. Submit Your Ballot

Follow the instructions in your email to submit your ballot.

In-Person Voting

Location: Congregation Beth Israel
1630 Bancroft Way, Berkeley

Date: Sunday, February 23rd
Time: 10:00 AM - 2:00 PM

What to Bring

Bring a valid ID and (if not on your ID) proof of residence in Assembly District 14.

At the Location

You'll be able to register and vote at the same time. Community volunteers will be there to help guide you through the process. If possible, it's best to register in advance at www.ademelections.com.

Why Your Vote Matters

Shape Progressive Policy

ADEMs help shape the California Democratic Party platform through direct participation and voting. Your vote ensures the party champions meaningful solutions for housing affordability, climate action, and community needs while staying true to progressive values.

Advocate for Local Communities

Your ADEMs connect Assembly District 14's needs with state party resources and attention. From affordable housing to infrastructure improvements, we're committed to bringing progressive solutions to Berkeley, Richmond, Albany, and our neighboring cities.

Support Progressive Champions

ADEMs play a crucial role in endorsing Democratic candidates. By electing our slate, you help ensure support for candidates who combine progressive values with practical experience in delivering positive change for our communities.

Strengthen Our Democratic Party

This is your opportunity to help build a more effective and responsive Democratic Party. We'll work to ensure the party stays true to its progressive roots while focusing on practical solutions to make real progress on housing, climate, and social justice.
